# GlowFit

GlowFit is a wellness and nutrition tracking web app built specifically for Indian lifestyles. It helps you stay on top of your health by tracking what you eat, how much water you drink, how well you sleep, and how active you are — all from one clean, mobile-friendly interface.

Built with simplicity in mind. No subscriptions, no clutter. Just open it and start tracking.

**Live → https://glowfit-76f13.web.app**

---

## What it does

- Track daily calories and macros with an Indian food database
- Log water intake with customisable reminders
- Record workouts and steps
- Monitor sleep quality and duration
- View weekly progress through charts and stats
- Maintain daily streaks to build consistency
- Sign in with Google to sync your data across devices
